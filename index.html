About Me

I am a Turing Scholar studying Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin. Within Computer Science, I am interested in web development, machine learning, and security.


Last summer, I interned at the NASA Ames Research Center. Along with two graduate students, I modeled airplane accidents in SysML (Systems Modelling Language) and developed a framework that allows researchers to estimate the safety of aerospace systems.


In my free time, I'm probably engrossed in a mystery novel, winning board games, or trying not to fall off of a rock climbing wall.
